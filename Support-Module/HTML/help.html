General overview

OpenWeather is a team of IT experts and data scientists that has been practising deep weather data science since 2014. For each point on the globe, OpenWeather provides historical, current and forecasted weather data via light-speed APIs.

The company has a holding structure that consists of a parent company, Openweather Ltd. (headquartered in London), and its subsidiaries OpenWeatherMap Inc. (incorporated in the USA) and OpenWeatherMap SIA (incorporated in the EU, in Latvia).

As of 2020, OpenWeather Ltd. has more than 2.4 million customers and supports 2,000 new subscribers per day. This includes Fortune 500 companies and thousands of other businesses globally


How to use the Application

1) Open the application

2) Search the place in Search bar

3) Data will fetch from API 

4) See the Today and next 5 days forecasting.
